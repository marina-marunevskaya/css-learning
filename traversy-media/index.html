Where to start

How to start?

One of ways to start is with courses and tutorial videos. Nowadays there are a lot of free resources for different level of developers.

Examples of resources

  • Scrimba (Introduction to CSS)
  • Traversy Media (CSS Crash Course For Absolute Beginners)

Roadmaps

Roadmaps are a great resource of information regarding topics that are worth learning when you begin your path as a developer.

Examples of roadmaps

  • Kamran Ahmed (Web Developer Roadmap)
  • Traversy Media (Web Development In 2020 - A Practical Guide)

What's next?

One of the best ways to learn is practice. Watching videos or reading tutorials won't make you a professional developer. Information from videos or tutorials should be applied to be really beneficial.

After completing free courses you can try any paid course. One of the reasons to do so is to get a certificate than you can later provide when applying as a developer. I can't recommend any particular paid course because it's individual for every person. But I can give examples of sites where you can find paid courses.

Examples of sites with paid courses

  • Scrimba
  • Udemy
* - if you are not familiar with HTML, it's recommended to learn HTML basics first.
